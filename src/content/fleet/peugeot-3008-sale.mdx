Peugeot's clever crossover — a 2.0 HDi diesel under the bonnet driving the
front wheels, plus an electric motor on the rear axle that engages
on-demand for AWD or for short electric-only city runs (the ZEV mode on
the centre console).

Four driving modes via the rotary on the console: **Auto** (the system
picks the best blend of diesel and electric), **Sport** (both work
together for max torque), **4WD** (forces electric motor at the rear for
grip), **ZEV** (pure electric, short range, useful in town). Real
diesel-electric AWD in a 5-seat package — uncommon and useful on Cypriot
back roads.

Other spec worth knowing: full panoramic glass roof, automatic
transmission, cloth + leather sport seats, dual-zone climate. Cyprus
registration. RHD as standard for this market.
