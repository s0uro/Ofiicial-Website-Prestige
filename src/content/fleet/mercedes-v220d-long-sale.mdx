this season. Right-hand drive, which suits Cyprus perfectly. Spec is the
executive interior the Japanese market got: captain's chairs in the middle

tailgate, tow bar fitted.

Detailed in our bay before listing — paint clayed and polished, leather

registration. Pre-purchase inspection welcome.
