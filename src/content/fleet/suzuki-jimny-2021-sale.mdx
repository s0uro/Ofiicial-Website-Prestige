The Jimny that everyone wanted and very few got. Kinetic Yellow exterior,
ladder-frame chassis, selectable 4WD with proper low range, and a 4-speed
automatic gearbox that just works. One owner from new, Cyprus-registered,
RHD as standard for this market.

**Why this car is different.** The 2018-2024 Jimny was the last passenger
version of Suzuki's pure off-roader before the EU emissions rules pushed
it into commercial-only sale. So a clean, low-mileage automatic example
is the kind of car that holds value rather than depreciates — and there
will not be a direct successor.

**Spec at a glance.** Touchscreen infotainment with Apple CarPlay and
Android Auto, reversing camera, cruise control, climate control,
hill-hold, hill descent control, full-size spare on the tailgate, tow bar
fitted. The full-extra package by local standards — nothing missing.

**Off-road kit that works.** AllGrip Pro 4WD with two-speed transfer case
— rotate the lever for 2H (rear-wheel drive on the road), 4H (4WD high
range for sand or wet trails), 4L (4WD low range for crawling). Brake-LSD
traction control on each axle. Approach, departure, and breakover angles
are class-leading because the chassis is honest ladder-frame, not a
unibody crossover pretending to be a 4x4.

**Detailed in our bay before listing.** Paint clayed and polished,
headlights checked, interior biological-cleaned, drivetrain inspected.
Service history available on request. Pre-purchase inspection welcome.
