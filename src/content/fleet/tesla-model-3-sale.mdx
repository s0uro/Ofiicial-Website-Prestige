The Highland facelift arrived in 2024. Smoother nose, frameless windows,
quieter cabin, no driver instrument cluster (Tesla moved everything to
the central 15.4 inch screen). This is the **Long Range** RWD with the
bigger battery, real world 450+ km per charge in mixed Cyprus driving.

**Critical for the Cyprus market:** this is a European spec car, *not* a
Japanese import. That matters because charging just works: the connector
and on board charger match the EU and Cyprus public networks, with no
adapters and no half speed sessions. Navigation has Cyprus map data and
traffic out of the box, with Supercharger routing. The Tesla app pairs
cleanly with EU accounts, with no region locking. Software updates and
warranty service flow through Tesla's European channel, with no orphan
import gotchas.

Energy consumption is exactly what you read on Tesla's data sheet,
typically 14 to 16 kWh per 100km in mixed driving, less than €4 per 100km on
Cypriot residential electricity. About a quarter of the running cost of an
equivalent petrol sedan.

Detailed in our bay before listing: paint clayed and polished, glass
coated, interior deep cleaned, frunk and trunk seals checked. Service
history tracked through Tesla's app, available on screen during a
viewing.
