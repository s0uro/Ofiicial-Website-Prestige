The W212 E-Class facelift, the one with the four headlights and the
single bar grille that brought the model in line with the C-Class and the
S-Class of that era. Avantgarde line, chrome window surrounds, the proper
big three pointed star up front.

**The drivetrain.** 2.1 litre OM651 four cylinder turbodiesel, 204 hp,
500 Nm of torque, paired with Mercedes' 7G-Tronic Plus seven speed
automatic. This combination is one of the most tested drivetrains
Mercedes ever shipped, designed for German autobahn distance and
European taxi fleets. 248,000 km is normal mid life on this engine, not
end of life.

**Inside.** Black leather throughout, wood interior trim on the
dashboard and centre console, multimedia head unit with reversing camera,
dual zone climate, cruise, rear seat air vents, Bluetooth audio.
Avantgarde grade fit.

**Provenance.** Two keys, full service history available on request.
Cyprus registered, right hand drive. The kind of paperwork buyers want to
see when the mileage is six digits.

**Detailed in our bay before listing.** Paint clayed and polished,
headlights checked, leather conditioned, interior biological cleaned,
underside inspected. Pre purchase inspection welcome.
