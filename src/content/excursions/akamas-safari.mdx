Our most booked safari. A full day across the Akamas peninsula — the only
part of Cyprus still mostly off the grid. The route mixes short walks (not
hikes), a Blue Lagoon swim, and time for lunch at Latchi before the run back.

Bring swimwear, a hat and sun cream. The 4x4 has space for a small cooler
if you prefer to pack lunch.
